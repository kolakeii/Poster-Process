Process Work

Stage 1

First I planned to create a web poster that is shaped like the radiation
symbol, with the main text centered in the circle. The symbol itself would
be made out of div shapes.

Stage 2

Instead of portraying a hostile impression of this seminar, I decided to
switch to a GIF of an atom, which was procured from the internet. The
layout was made simple as a poster, with the negative space at the bottom
being reserved for the upper half of the atom gif.

Stage 3

I decided then to comibine both ideas, creating the first radiation symbol
out of shape divs, while changing the colour scheme to appear less hostile
